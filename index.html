History of Osmania Biscuits

Osmania Biscuits were first made in Hyderabad during the era of Mir Osman Ali Khan. They are famous for their unique sweet-salty taste and crispy texture. Raghuveer Osmania Biscuits brings this traditional taste to Maharashtra with special ingredients and care.
